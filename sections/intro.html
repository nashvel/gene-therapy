Introduction to Genetic Medicine

Definition

Gene therapy is the introduction of genes into existing cells to prevent or cure a wide range of diseases.

It is a technique for correcting defective genes responsible for disease development.

Gene Delivery

A gene cannot be directly inserted into a cell. It requires a carrier or vector.

Viral Vectors

The most common vectors are genetically modified viruses that carry normal human DNA.

How It Works

Viruses have evolved to encapsulate and transport genes to human cells efficiently.

What is a Gene?

Every single trait you have, from your hair color to your height, is controlled by the code found in your genes.

They're the secret code that makes you uniquely you!
