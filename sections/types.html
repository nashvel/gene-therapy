Two Types of Gene Therapy

Somatic Cell Gene Therapy

For the Individual

  • Manipulation of genes in body cells
  • Helpful to the patient
  • Not inherited by the next generation
  • Used in current treatments
  • Considered ethically acceptable
Changes affect only the treated individual
Germ-line Gene Therapy

For Future Generations

  • Genetic modification of germ cells
  • Changes to egg or sperm cells
  • Inherited by next generation
  • Can eliminate genetic diseases from family lines
  • Raises ethical concerns
Permanent changes passed to offspring
